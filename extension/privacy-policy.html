Privacy Policy - Synapse Chrome Extension

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

Synapse Chrome Extension ("we", "our", "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Extension.

Information We Collect

What We Collect:

  • Webpage Content: Page titles, URLs, and content that you choose to save
  • Highlighted Text: Text snippets you select and save
  • Notes: Notes and annotations you create
  • Metadata: Save timestamps and source URLs

What We DON'T Collect:

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • ❌ Browsing history (unless you actively save it)
  • ❌ Data from other websites
  • ❌ Device information or location data
  • ❌ Analytics or tracking data

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your Synapse backend server (localhost:8001)
  • Data is NOT uploaded to our servers
  • Data is NOT shared with third parties
  • You can delete all data at any time

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Display your saved content in Synapse
  • Provide search and organization features
  • Generate AI summaries (if enabled by you)

Third-Party Services

The Extension may use the following services:

  • OpenAI API: For generating summaries (optional feature)
    • Only used when you configure your API key
    • You control whether this feature is enabled

We do NOT use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Social media integrations

Cookies and Tracking

  • The Extension does NOT use cookies
  • The Extension does NOT use any tracking technologies
  • The Extension does NOT contain advertisements

Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • ✅ View all saved data
  • ✅ Delete any saved content
  • ✅ Export your data
  • ✅ Completely uninstall the Extension

Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab when you click the Extension
  • storage: To save Extension settings
  • contextMenus: To add right-click menu options

We do NOT request:

  • Access to all websites
  • Access to your browsing history
  • Access to your personal data

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified in the Extension's update notes.
